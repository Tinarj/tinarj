I am currently a **PhD student at Monash University**, working on **shape analysis**, **statistical methods**, and **data visualization**.

I am interested in how statistical and computational tools can help us understand complex data, especially when the data has geometric structure, high dimensionality, or visual patterns that are not easy to describe with standard methods.

I enjoy contributing to **R packages**, creating tools that make research and analysis easier, and sharing knowledge through **teaching**.

Outside research and coding, I love **taking pictures**, and **cooking**.
